About Me

Data Science Journey

The spark for my journey into data science ignited during my collaboration with data scientists at Mutual of Omaha. Witnessing their transformative capabilities left me inspired, prompting me to explore how data science could enhance my role.

Education

I hold a master's degree in data science from Bellevue University, where I delved into the realms of Python, R, and various machine learning models. This academic journey equipped me with the tools to bridge the gap between data analysis and predictive analytics.

Career Goals

I thrive on the challenge of pushing the boundaries of what data can reveal and contributing to strategic decision-making.
